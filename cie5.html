Breaking News: UGC grants autonomous status to SMVITM

.The University Grants Commission (UGC) has granted autonomous status to Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, a unit of Sode Group of Institutions, Udupi. It is effective for 10 years from the academic year 2025-26.

Students of SMVITM won the prizes in Table Tennis Tournament

Students of Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management (SMVITM), Bantakal, showcased their sporting excellence by winning prizes in the VTU Mangaluru Division Table Tennis Tournament 2025-26, held at Srinivas Institute of Technology, Valachil, Mangaluru, on 15th and 16th September 2025.
